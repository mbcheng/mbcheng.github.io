Hello! My name is Michelle Cheng.

I often report on higher education, technology and social issues. I also like using compelling data analysis and data viz to further tell a story. I graduated from Boston University with a degree in journalism and a concentration in computer science. Prior to becoming a journalist, I was a competitive dancer.

I'm currently an interactive producer at KQED News. My work has been featured in FiveThirtyEight, Forbes, MIT Technology Review and Upworthy.
