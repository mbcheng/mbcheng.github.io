    About Me

    Hello! I am a senior at Boston University studying journalism with a concentration in computer science and minoring in English (or possibly vice versa). My interest in journalism transformed from creating collages out of glossy magazine pages to writing about how people live and bringing awareness to topics like higher education, female empowerment and digital culture.

    After learning skills in visual design and coding, I became interested in data-driven stories where analysis, writing and data visualizations combine to actively engage readers. I like digging deep for my stories and constantly challenging myself to becoming a better storyteller in the digital age.

    My work has been published in Forbes, USA TODAY College, Mochi Magazine, Internet Week New York and The Quad.
