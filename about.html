Journalist; Digital Storyteller

My name is Michelle Cheng, and I am a junior at Boston University studying journalism with a concentration in computer science and minoring in English. My interest in journalism transformed from creating collages out of glossy magazine pages to telling the stories of how people live and bringing awareness to issues that matter, particularly in higher education.

After learning skills in visual design and coding, I became interested in data-driven stories where well-written content and data visualization combine to actively engage readers. I like digging deep for my stories and constantly challenging myself to becoming a better storyteller in the digital age.
